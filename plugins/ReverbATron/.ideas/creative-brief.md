# Reverb-a-tron

## Concept
A harmonically intelligent reverb that listens to what you play and tunes its resonances to match. Combines four unique features: harmonic resonance (room rings in key with the input), frequency sculpting (shape the reverb tail independently from the dry signal), flutter (organic warbling reflections that add movement), and beat sync (tempo-locked decay and modulation). The room becomes a musical instrument.

## Target User
Producers & beatmakers — electronic, hip-hop, pop. People who want creative, rhythmic, musical reverb effects that go beyond static room simulation.

## Key Differentiator
No other reverb combines pitch-tracking resonance with beat-synced decay, spectral sculpting, and flutter modulation. The space responds musically to the input rather than passively processing it.

## Audio Characteristics
Reverb tails that harmonize with the input using FFT-based dominant-pitch detection, rhythmically pulse with the beat, shimmer with organic flutter modulation, and can be spectrally sculpted to sit perfectly in a mix. Works best on tonal material with a clear fundamental.
